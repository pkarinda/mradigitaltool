About the EAC
Mutual Recognition Portal

The East African Community (EAC) is committed to enabling the free movement of labor within its Partner States by harmonizing and mutually recognizing academic and professional qualifications. However, despite the existence of the Mutual Recognition Agreement (MRA) for engineers, professionals frequently encounter obstacles when trying to have their licenses acknowledged across borders. The primary issue is the absence of a systematic process for the exchange of information between the Competent Authorities (CAs) responsible for issuing and recognizing professional licenses.

This deficiency creates significant challenges and delays in the issuance of mutual recognition credentials, making it difficult for engineers to practice in different EAC Partner States. The EAC Secretariat's innovative digital tool aims to address these challenges by enhancing the recognition and mobility of professional engineers throughout the region. This initiative is a crucial step towards achieving greater regional integration and cooperation among the Partner States.
